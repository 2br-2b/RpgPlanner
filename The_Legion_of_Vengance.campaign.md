## Hold the hill
**Type:** mission

### Overview
#### Background
Your opponents are targeting a specific base. Your mission is to hold out long enough for air support to get in.
Each turn, the enemy will send more units into the map, rolling each turn to see which side, etc. After a certain number of turns, friendly air support will arrive. You need to hold off the invaders and destroy them all!


### Setup
#### Map
Set up a ring of cliffs, and a few buildings in the middle. This is your base. You spawn in the middle of the ring.
#### Waypoints
Put a waypoint A-G on each hex from 1-7. Put Waypoint H onto space 10.


### Special Rules
The enemy units follow forced withdrawal rules.

### End Conditions
You win if:
- Waypoint H has been revealed, and no enemy units remain on the map
- At the start of turn 7, all enemy units have been eliminated

You lose if:
- The base is destroyed

### Waypoints
Waypoint Alpha
Waypoint Bravo
Waypoint Charlie
Waypoint Delta - A respite! Are they planning something or just regrouping? Nothing happens this turn.
Waypoint Echo
Waypoint Foxtrot - the friendly aerospace forces arrive! Each turn, you get X heavy bombings.
Waypoint Golf
Waypoint Hotel - your tireless bombardment has tired out the enemy! All enemy units are now in forced withdrawal
